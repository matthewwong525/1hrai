# Monetization Plan

## YouTube Partner Program Requirements

| Tier | Requirements | Unlocks |
|------|-------------|---------|
| **Tier 1 (Early)** | 500 subs + 3 videos in 90 days + 3K watch hours or 3M Shorts views | Memberships, Super Chat, Shopping |
| **Tier 2 (Full)** | 1,000 subs + 4K watch hours or 10M Shorts views | Ad revenue (55% long-form / 45% Shorts) |

## CPM Context

AI tools/automation sits in Tier 1 CPM ($15-$100+) — one of the highest-paying niches. This means fewer views are needed to generate meaningful revenue compared to gaming ($1-$3 CPM).

### CPM by Audience Geography

| Country | Average CPM |
|---------|-------------|
| Australia | ~$36 |
| United States | ~$33 |
| UK/Canada | $15-25 |
| India | <$1 |

**Implication:** Content targeting English-speaking business owners in US/AU/UK maximizes CPM.

## Revenue Diversification Path (Recommended Order)

1. **Affiliate links** — Add immediately; near-zero effort (2-50% commissions). AI tools have strong affiliate programs.
2. **YouTube ad revenue** — Join YPP when eligible
3. **Channel memberships** — Available at 500 subs; predictable recurring revenue
4. **Email list** — Start from day 1; your algorithm-proof direct line. Email subscribers convert at 5-10x the rate of social followers.
5. **Simple digital product** — Once you understand audience pain points (highest margins). For 1HR AI: templates, workflow guides, prompt libraries.
6. **Sponsorships** — Once you have consistent viewership data to present. AI/SaaS sponsors pay well.
7. **Courses/premium products** — Once proven demand exists (5-10x ad revenue potential)
8. **Merchandise** — Once you have strong brand identity

**The principle:** Relying solely on AdSense is fragile. The most successful creators have 4-6 income streams, with ad revenue often representing only 20-30% of total earnings.

## The "1,000 True Fans" Model

Modern creators may need only 100-300 superfans at $300-500 lifetime value each. Build a layered model: free content (YouTube) -> community (Discord/memberships) -> premium offerings (courses, 1-on-1, exclusive content).

## When to Quit Your Day Job

Prerequisites (subscriber count alone is misleading):
- 6-12 months of cash runway saved
- Healthcare plan secured
- Diversified income (never rely on AdSense alone)
- 6+ months of consistent revenue exceeding expenses
- Tax planning — remember the 15.3% self-employment tax plus lost employer benefits
- High-CPM niches (finance, tech, B2B) can do it with far fewer subscribers — sometimes 50K-200K
